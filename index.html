Wowie zowie, life with anxiety sure does

suck!

But it can be managed, says my therapist!
Let's take a peek at some ideas:

Even if you feel like you're dying, the feeling will pass~
Validate your own emotions; they are telling you something!
Allowing yourself to feel your feelings is healthy!
If you follow your emotions with curiosity, it slowly gives way to the why and permits them to take up the space they need, and soon they will begin to bother you less.
Your worth is not determined by your productivity~
People in your life love you!

My favored methods of calming down
Type of Exercise How It Works
Deep breathing Box breathing: inhale for a count of 4, hold for a count of 4, exhale for a count of 4, hold for a count of four. Repeat!
Child's pose (yoga) Kneel on the ground, keeping your toes together and knees apart, sit your hips back and lean forward, placing your hands on the floor over your head. Breathe deeply, feeling your belly press against your thighs, and continue breathing in and out. Maintain this posture until you feel better.
Affirmations Repeat aloud to yourself: I am safe. I have perserverence. I have courage. I have confidence. I have enough time.
Squishmallows They are cute and you can tell them your secrets and give 'em hugs, and when you get frustrated, they don't mind if you shake 'em a little (or a lot).
